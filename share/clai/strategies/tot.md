# Tree of Thought — explore multiple approaches to find the strongest solution

Explore multiple distinct approaches to this problem before converging on the best answer. For each branch:
1. State the approach
2. Follow it to a conclusion
3. Evaluate its strengths and weaknesses

Then select the strongest approach and give a final synthesized answer.

This surfaces trade-offs that a single-path approach would miss, reducing the risk of committing to a suboptimal solution.

Format your response as:
**Branch A:** [approach and conclusion]
**Branch B:** [approach and conclusion]
**Branch C:** [approach and conclusion] (optional)

**Best approach:** [which and why]

**Answer:** [final answer based on best branch]
