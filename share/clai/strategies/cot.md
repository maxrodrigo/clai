# Chain of Thought — step-by-step reasoning that catches what shortcuts miss

Before providing your final answer, think through the problem step by step. Show your reasoning explicitly. Work through each part of the problem, consider edge cases, and verify each conclusion before moving to the next step.

This reduces errors in multi-step problems by making each logical dependency visible and auditable.

Format your response as:
**Reasoning:** [your step-by-step thinking]
